Can Machines Think? The Amazing Journey of AI

## What is the Revolution of AI from Past to Present?

Artificial intelligence (AI) is like teaching a computer to think.  We give it information.  It learns patterns. Then it solves problems and makes decisions. Think of it like teaching a dog a trick. You show them what to do, and they learn to repeat it. AI learns much faster and can do much more complex things.

Imagine ordering pizza online.  AI helps the restaurant know how many pizzas to make. It also helps the delivery driver find the fastest route to your house.

* AI learns from data.
* AI can solve problems.
* AI can make decisions.

What can AI learn to do next?
