# About facebook

## What is facebook?

Facebook is a social media platform.  It connects people online. Think of it like a giant digital yearbook. You can share photos and updates with friends.  You can also join groups based on your interests.  Ever wanted to find old classmates? Facebook helps you do that.  It’s a way to stay in touch with people near and far. What if you could share your birthday party with everyone at once?  Facebook makes that possible.

* Connects people online
* Shares photos and updates
* Helps you find friends and family
* Creates online communities

## How Did We Get Here?

Facebook started small.  Mark Zuckerberg created it in 2004.  It was only for college students at first.  Can you imagine? Now, billions of people use it. Facebook grew quickly. It added features like messaging and video calls.  Twenty years ago, connecting with people online was harder.  Now, it's easy with Facebook.  Do you remember life before social media?

* **2004:** Facebook launches for college students.
* **2006:** Facebook opens to everyone.
* **2012:** Facebook buys Instagram.
* **2014:** Facebook buys WhatsApp.
* **2020s:**  Facebook focuses on the "metaverse".


## Why It Matters to Everyone

Facebook impacts our daily lives. It changes how we communicate. Businesses use it to reach customers.  News spreads quickly on Facebook.  It’s not just for fun.  People organize events and raise money for good causes. Have you ever learned about something important on Facebook?  It’s a powerful tool for sharing information.

* Connects businesses with customers
* Helps people organize events
* Spreads news and information
* Facilitates communication


## Real-World Proof

Facebook's influence is everywhere. It's a major part of our digital world.  Researchers study its effects on society.  Governments consider its power.  Would you believe Facebook impacts elections?  It’s a force to be reckoned with.
