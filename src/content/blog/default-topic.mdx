# About facebook

## What is facebook?

Facebook is a social media platform.  It connects people online. Think of it like a giant digital yearbook.  You can share photos and updates. You can chat with friends and family.  Ever wanted to see what your old classmates are up to? Facebook makes it easy.  You can join groups based on your interests.  What are your hobbies? There's probably a Facebook group for it!

* Connects people online
* Shares photos and updates
* Hosts groups and communities

## How Did We Get Here?

Facebook started small.  Mark Zuckerberg created it in 2004.  At first, it was only for college students.  Can you imagine Facebook just for your school?  It quickly grew to include everyone. Now, billions of people use Facebook.

* **2004:** Facebook launches for college students.
* **2006:** Facebook opens to everyone over 13.
* **2012:** Facebook reaches one billion users.
* **Present:** Facebook is a global communication tool.  How has it changed your life?

## Why It Matters to Everyone

Facebook impacts our daily lives.  It changes how we get news. It connects us with people far away. Businesses use it to reach customers.  It's a powerful tool for communication.  Do you use Facebook to connect with family?  It's become a part of our modern world.

* Connects people globally
* Shares news and information
* Helps businesses reach customers

## Real-World Proof

Facebook is a real platform used by billions. It has changed communication.  It connects people across the globe. It’s a powerful tool for sharing information.  How will social media evolve next?
