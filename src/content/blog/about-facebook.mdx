# About facebook

## What is facebook?

Facebook is a social media platform.  Think of it as a giant online yearbook.  It connects people worldwide.  You create a profile.  You share photos and updates. You can chat with friends and family.  Ever wanted to share vacation photos instantly? Facebook makes it easy.  You can join groups about your hobbies.  You can follow businesses and organizations.  Have you ever found an old friend online?  Facebook helps you reconnect.

* Connects people worldwide
* Share updates and photos
* Join groups and follow pages

What if you could celebrate a birthday with friends across the globe?  Facebook makes this possible.


## How Did We Get Here?

Facebook started small.  Mark Zuckerberg created it in 2004.  It was just for college students at first.  Then it grew quickly.  Now, billions of people use it.

* **2004:** Facebook launches for college students.
* **2006:** Facebook opens to everyone over 13.
* **2012:** Facebook reaches one billion users.

Years ago, sharing news took time.  Now, it’s instant.  Facebook changed how we communicate.  Can you imagine life without it?


## Why It Matters to Everyone

Facebook impacts our daily lives.  Businesses use it to advertise.  Families stay connected across distances.  News spreads rapidly.  It’s a powerful tool.  We can learn new things.  We can support causes we care about. Have you ever learned about a local event on Facebook?

* Businesses connect with customers.
* Families stay in touch.
* News travels quickly.

Would you use Facebook to organize a community event?  Why or why not?
