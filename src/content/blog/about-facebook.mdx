# About facebook

## What is facebook?

Facebook is a social media platform.  It connects people online. Think of it like a giant digital yearbook.  You can share photos and updates. You can also chat with friends and family.  Ever wanted to share vacation pictures quickly? Facebook makes it easy.  It's like a global community in your pocket.  What if you could instantly reconnect with old classmates? Facebook lets you do just that.

* Connects people online.
* Shares photos and updates.
* Enables chatting with friends and family.


## How Did We Get Here?

Facebook started small.  Mark Zuckerberg created it in 2004.  It was only for college students at first.  Can you believe that? Now, billions of people use it.  

* **2004:** Facebook launches for college students.
* **2006:** Facebook opens to everyone over 13.
* **2012:** Facebook buys Instagram.
* **2014:** Facebook buys WhatsApp.

Years ago, connecting with people far away took time. Now, it's instant.  How has Facebook changed how you communicate?


## Why It Matters to Everyone

Facebook affects many parts of our lives.  Businesses use it to connect with customers.  Families use it to stay in touch.  News spreads quickly on Facebook.  It can be a powerful tool for sharing information.  Do you think Facebook helps people stay connected?  It also raises questions about privacy. It's important to think about how we use it.

* Connects businesses with customers.
* Helps families stay in touch.
* Spreads news and information.
